About Us

We established a Dental Purchasing Service (DPS) in 2011 to help a group of five dental clinics with their procurement needs. The primary goal was to leverage the collective buying power of these clinics to negotiate better prices and deals with vendors. By representing the group and managing their procurement requirements, we aimed to create a win-win situation for both the clinics and the vendors.

Our approach involves working with multiple vendors and manufacturers to secure the best possible prices for the products and supplies needed by the dental clinics. This approach allows us to tailor the product selection to the specific needs of each clinic, ensuring that they receive the most suitable and cost-effective items. Furthermore, all products are hand-selected by a DPS agent, indicating a personalized and quality-focused approach to procurement.

Overall, Dental Purchasing Service provides an efficient and cost-effective solution for the group of clinics, helping them streamline their procurement processes and obtain the best value for their supplies and equipment. This model has been successful since its inception in 2011, indicating its effectiveness in serving the needs of dental practices.
